Parts of Speech

Angrezi zubaan mein lafzon ki 8 bunyaadi iqsamaat. In ko "PANIC VAC" se yaad rakhein.

Noun

Ism / Naam

A word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.

Kisi bhi shakhs, cheez, jagah ya khayaal ka naam.

Pronoun

Zameer

A word used in place of a noun.

Ism ki jagah istemaal hone wala lafz.

Adjective

Sifat

A word that describes a noun or pronoun.

Ism ya Zameer ki khaasiyat bayan karta hai.

Verb

Fe'l / Amal

A word that shows action or state of being.

Kaam ya haalat ko zahir karta hai.

Adverb

Mutabiq-e-sifat

A word that modifies a verb, adjective, or another adverb.

Fe'l, Sifat ya dosray Mutabiq-e-sifat ko mazeed wazeh karta hai.

Preposition

Harf-e-jarr

A word showing relationship between words.

Do lafzon ke darmiyaan rishta bayan karta hai.

Conjunction

Harf-e-rabt

A word that joins words or sentences.

Do alag-alag jumlon ya lafzon ko jorhta hai.

Interjection

Nidaa / Izhar-e-jazbaat

A word that expresses strong emotion.

Achank nikli hui aawaaz ya jazbaat ka izhaar.

12 English Tenses

Har tense ka formula, example aur common mistakes.

Present Tenses (Haal)

Subject + V1 (+s/es)

Use: Aadat, haqeeqat, ya rozmarra ke kaam.

Galti: He/She/It ke saath s/es lagana mat bhooliye!
I eat breakfast.
Main nashta karta hoon.

Subject + is/am/are + V+ing

She is studying.
Woh parh rahi hai.

Subject + have/has + V3

Use: Kaam ho chuka hai lekin waqt nahi bataya.

I have eaten lunch.
Main dopahar ka khana kha chuka hoon.

Subject + have/has been + V+ing

Use: Kaam kuch waqt se jaari hai.

She has been waiting for 2 hours.
Woh 2 ghantay se intezaar kar rahi hai.

Past Tenses (Maazi)

Subject + V2

I went to Karachi.
Main Karachi gaya.

Subject + was/were + V+ing

Use: Kaam maazi mein jaari tha.

They were playing cricket.
Woh cricket khel rahe thay.

Subject + had + V3

Use: Ek kaam doosre se pehle ho chuka tha.

I had finished before he came.
Main us ke aane se pehle khatam kar chuka tha.

Subject + had been + V+ing

Use: Kaam kuch waqt se jaari tha (kisi event se pehle).

She had been studying for 3 hours.
Woh 3 ghantay se parh rahi thi.

Future Tenses (Mustaqbil)

Subject + will + V1

We will win the match.
Hum match jeet jayenge.

Subject + will be + V+ing

Use: Kaam mustaqbil mein jaari hoga.

I will be working at 8 PM.
Main raat 8 bajay kaam kar raha hounga.

Subject + will have + V3

Use: Kaam mustaqbil mein kisi waqt tak ho chuka hoga.

She will have left by then.
Woh tab tak ja chuki hogi.

Subject + will have been + V+ing

Use: Kaam kuch waqt se jaari hoga mustaqbil mein.

By 2027, I will have been living here for 10 years.
2027 tak, main yahan 10 saal se reh raha hounga.

Active vs Passive

Focusing on the action rather than the doer.

TenseActivePassive
Present SimpleHe writes a letter.A letter is written by him.
Present ContinuousHe is writing a letter.A letter is being written by him.
Present PerfectHe has written a letter.A letter has been written by him.
Past SimpleHe wrote a letter.A letter was written by him.
Past ContinuousHe was writing a letter.A letter was being written by him.
Future SimpleHe will write a letter.A letter will be written by him.

Passive Formula: Object + be (tense) + V3 + by + Subject
